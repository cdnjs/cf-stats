## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **over 251 billion requests** in May 2022. ðŸ–¥
- cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 4.67 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. ðŸ“¤
- That works out to **nearly 151 TB of data and 8.1 billion requests every day** (averaged). ðŸ¤¯
- In May, **each request to cdnjs used only 18.62 KB of data on average**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 19.33 billion requests for jquery,
    800.52 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 15.47 billion requests and consumed 496.80 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 11.20 billion requests and
    using 60.37 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 9.60 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.02 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 11.20 billion requests and using 60.37 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    7.28 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.62 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,510,945,786 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 251,094,578,600 requests in May.

**Over 251 billion requests or roughly 8.1 billion requests every single day of May**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 46,743.58 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 4,674,358.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 4.67 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 4.67 petabytes of data for requests this month**. ðŸ¤¯
