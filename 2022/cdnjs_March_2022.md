Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (March 2022), cdnjs served **just under 241 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 4.56 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **over 147 TB of data and 7.8 billion requests each day** (averaged). 🤯
- In March, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 18.93 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 18.14 billion requests for jquery,
    763.79 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 16.45 billion requests and consumed 513.13 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 11.44 billion requests and
    using 61.66 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 9.68 billion requests, and
    jquery-mousewheel in 5th with 4.05 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 11.44 billion requests and using 61.66 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    7.52 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.69 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,408,828,385 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 240,882,838,500 requests in March.

**Just under 241 billion requests or around 7.8 billion requests each day of March**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 45,597.05 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 4,559,705.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 4.56 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 4.56 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
