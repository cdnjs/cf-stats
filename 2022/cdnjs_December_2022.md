## Key highlights

- This month, cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 5.18 PB of data** to serve these requests. ðŸ“¤
- That's **over 167 terabytes of data and 8.3 billion requests every day** (averaged). ðŸ¤¯
- In December, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 20.21 KB of data**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and gsap.
  - Across the 21.76 billion requests for jquery,
    898.21 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 16.91 billion requests and consumed 524.41 TB
    of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100 getting 10.77 billion requests and
    using 301.59 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 10.64 billion requests, and
    slick-carousel in 5th with 3.74 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 10.64 billion requests and using 56.68 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    7.25 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.90 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,563,780,010 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 256,378,001,000 requests in December.

**Over 256 billion requests or around 8.3 billion requests each day of December**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 51,819.39 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,181,939.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.18 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.18 petabytes of data for requests in December**. ðŸ¤¯
