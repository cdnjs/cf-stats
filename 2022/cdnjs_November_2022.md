Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (November 2022), cdnjs served **nearly 263 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 5.19 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **over 173 TB of data and 8.8 billion requests each day** (averaged). 🤯
- In November, **each request to cdnjs used only 19.77 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 19.85 billion requests for jquery,
    824.44 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 16.27 billion requests and consumed 517.29 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 13.35 billion requests and
    using 71.03 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 11.61 billion requests, and
    slick-carousel in 5th with 3.89 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 13.35 billion requests and using 71.03 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    7.19 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.86 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,625,871,946 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 262,587,194,600 requests in November.

**Nearly 263 billion requests or around 8.8 billion requests every single day of November**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 51,915.18 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,191,518.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.19 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 5.19 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
