- In March, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 23.06 KB of data**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 32.55 billion requests for jquery,
    1,531.09 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 18.94 billion requests and consumed 661.53 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 9.73 billion requests and
    using 49.09 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 8.51 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 5.30 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 13.76 billion requests and using 1,031.99 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    9.73 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) with 8.19 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,870,031,593 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 287,003,159,300 requests in March.

**Over 287 billion requests or approximately 9.3 billion requests each day of March**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 66,181.12 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,618,112.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.62 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 6.62 petabytes of data for requests this month**. ðŸ¤¯
