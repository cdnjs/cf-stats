Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (August 2024), cdnjs served **nearly 302 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 6.91 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That's **just under 223 terabytes of data and 9.7 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In August, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 22.90 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 33.41 billion requests for jquery,
    1,662.57 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 24.69 billion requests and consumed 1,031.25 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 9.67 billion requests and
    using 49.73 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 6.30 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 5.88 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 15.59 billion requests and using 1,188.43 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    9.67 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) with 7.67 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 3,016,898,189 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 301,689,818,900 requests in August.

**Nearly 302 billion requests or roughly 9.7 billion requests every single day of August**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 69,081.06 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,908,106.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.91 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 6.91 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
