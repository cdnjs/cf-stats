### Library highlights

 taking the lead, followed by jQuery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js).**

 requests.**

   seeing 9.9 billion requests for the 16 files in the top 100 resources for the month.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 1,849,204,306 at 1%.\
> This is 184,920,430,600 when multiplied up to 100%.
> 
> We are also given a number of requests for 3 days at a 100% sample, which is 18,344,643,906.\
> This is 183,446,439,060 when recalculated for the 30 days of June.

To provide the best possible estimate for the entire month, an average of both numbers will be used to generate the
 estimate for the final number of requests for the month (75%: 1% month sample data, 25%: 100% 3 day data).\
This results in cdnjs serving approximately 184,551,932,715 requests in June.

**Nearly 185 billion requests or approximately 6.2 billion requests every single day of June**. ðŸ“ˆ\
Similar to last month, there was a slight drop in cdnjs requests overall this month. However, the average requests per
 day slightly crept up, from 6.1 billion per day last month to 6.2 billion this month. This still leaves us
 significantly ahead of where we were sixth months ago, when we saw closer to 160 billion requests per month.

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 32,256.44 GB.\
> This is 3,225,644.0 GB or 3.23 PB when multiplied up to be 100%.
> 
> Additionally, a 3 day 100% sample is given by Cloudflare at 330,024.33 GB.\
> This results in 3,300,243.3 GB or 3.30 PB for the month.

As with the total number of requests and due to the significant difference between the two, both numbers will be used to
 calculate an average for the final estimate of bandwidth consumed this month (75%: 1% month sample data, 25%: 100% 3
 day data).\
This produces the estimate of 3,244,293.8 GB of bandwidth used for this month by cdnjs.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 3.24 petabytes of data for requests in June**. ðŸ¤¯\
This month cdnjs saw a large bump in total bandwidth used compared to previous months, with a 15% increase seen from
 last month. cdnjs consumed 3.24 PB of bandwidth to serve requests this month, the largest consumption in a single month
 seen for cdnjs so far. This is similar to the spike seen in February of this year, and can likely be explained in part
 by the increase in requests to FontAwesome's font files.
