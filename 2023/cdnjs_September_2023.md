### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and gsap.
  - Across the 26.40 billion requests for jquery,
    1,198.25 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 16.38 billion requests and consumed 502.48 TB
    of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100 getting 9.73 billion requests and
    using 227.64 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 9.40 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.64 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 10.40 billion requests and using 768.90 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    9.40 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 6.09 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,702,941,935 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 270,294,193,500 requests in September.

**Over 270 billion requests or approximately 9.0 billion requests each day of September**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 57,714.4 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,771,440.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.77 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 5.77 petabytes of data for requests this month**. ðŸ¤¯
