### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 27.82 billion requests for jquery,
    1,200.16 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 17.05 billion requests and consumed 533.52 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 10.52 billion requests and
    using 56.13 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 10.12 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.46 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 10.52 billion requests and using 56.13 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    9.79 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) with 8.44 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,754,658,378 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 275,465,837,800 requests in May.

**Over 275 billion requests or around 8.9 billion requests every day of May**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 57,778.2 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,777,820.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.78 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.78 petabytes of data for requests this month**. ðŸ¤¯
