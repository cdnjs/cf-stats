### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 56.61 billion requests for jquery,
    2,506.93 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 33.91 billion requests and consumed 1,077.67 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 21.27 billion requests and
    using 111.73 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 17.52 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 9.31 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 10.63 billion requests and using 55.87 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    10.63 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) with 10.52 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,687,760,672 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 268,776,067,200 requests in July.

**Nearly 269 billion requests or around 8.7 billion requests every single day of July**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 56,559.91 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,655,991.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.66 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.66 petabytes of data for requests in July**. ðŸ¤¯
