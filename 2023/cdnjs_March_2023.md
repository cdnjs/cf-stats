Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (March 2023), cdnjs served **just under 273 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 5.63 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **nearly 182 TB of data and 8.8 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In March, **each request to cdnjs used only 20.64 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and gsap.
  - Across the 24.35 billion requests for jquery,
    1,050.73 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 17.35 billion requests and consumed 521.76 TB
    of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100 getting 10.35 billion requests and
    using 263.51 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.17 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.42 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 8.95 billion requests and using 640.10 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    7.17 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 6.20 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,729,252,314 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 272,925,231,400 requests in March.

**Just under 273 billion requests or around 8.8 billion requests every day of March**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 56,321.03 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,632,103.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.63 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.63 petabytes of data for requests in March**. 🤯
