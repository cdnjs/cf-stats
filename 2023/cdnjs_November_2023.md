# cdnjs November 2023 Usage Stats

Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

- [Key highlights](#key-highlights)

## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **over 262 billion requests** in November 2023. 🖥
- cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 5.69 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. 📤
- That's **nearly 190 terabytes of data and 8.7 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In November, **each request to cdnjs used only 21.72 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and gsap.
  - Across the 26.11 billion requests for jquery,
    1,231.27 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 16.12 billion requests and consumed 510.88 TB
    of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100 getting 10.18 billion requests and
    using 235.93 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 9.87 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.81 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 11.10 billion requests and using 830.17 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    9.87 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) with 5.86 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,620,291,475 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 262,029,147,500 requests in November.

**Over 262 billion requests or around 8.7 billion requests every single day of November**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 56,901.22 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,690,122.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.69 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.69 petabytes of data for requests in November**. 🤯
