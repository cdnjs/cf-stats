# cdnjs December 2023 Usage Stats

Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **over 276 billion requests** in December 2023. 🖥
- cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 6.14 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. 📤
- That's **just under 198 terabytes of data and 8.9 billion requests each day** (averaged). 🤯
- In December, **each request to cdnjs used only 22.21 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and gsap.
  - Across the 28.44 billion requests for jquery,
    1,364.46 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 17.80 billion requests and consumed 580.33 TB
    of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100 getting 10.65 billion requests and
    using 246.84 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 9.47 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 5.14 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 12.48 billion requests and using 940.37 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    9.47 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 6.20 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,762,565,707 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 276,256,570,700 requests in December.

**Over 276 billion requests or approximately 8.9 billion requests every day of December**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 61,354.81 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,135,481.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.14 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 6.14 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
