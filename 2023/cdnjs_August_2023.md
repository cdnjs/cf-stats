# cdnjs August 2023 Usage Stats

Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **over 274 billion requests** in August 2023. 🖥
- cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 5.81 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. 📤
- That works out to **over 187 TB of data and 8.9 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In August, **each request to cdnjs used only 21.16 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 27.42 billion requests for jquery,
    1,236.33 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 17.61 billion requests and consumed 599.55 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 10.85 billion requests and
    using 57.15 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 9.32 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 4.79 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 10.85 billion requests and using 57.15 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    10.65 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) with 6.42 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,744,355,744 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 274,435,574,400 requests in August.

**Over 274 billion requests or approximately 8.9 billion requests every single day of August**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 58,079.48 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 5,807,948.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 5.81 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 5.81 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
