## Key highlights

- This month (October 2025), cdnjs served **nearly 300 billion requests**. ðŸ–¥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 7.02 PB of data** to serve these requests. ðŸ“¤
- That works out to **over 226 TB of data and 9.7 billion requests every day** (averaged). ðŸ¤¯
- In October, **each request to cdnjs used only 23.41 KB of data on average**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and angular.js.
  - Across the 31.91 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,583.86 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 18.42 billion requests and consumed 489.38 TB
    of bandwidth, with angular.js assets in the top 100 getting 13.99 billion requests and
    using 177.78 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.93 billion requests, and
    gsap in 5th with 6.23 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of
  webfont, reaching a total of 7.93 billion requests and using 41.13 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    7.32 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.04 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,997,109,902 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 299,710,990,200 requests in October.

**Nearly 300 billion requests or approximately 9.7 billion requests each day of October**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 70,153.45 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 7,015,345.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 7.02 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 7.02 petabytes of data for requests in October**. ðŸ¤¯
