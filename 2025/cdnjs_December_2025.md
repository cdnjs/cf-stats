Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **nearly 302 billion requests** in December 2025. 🖥
- cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 7.16 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. 📤
- That works out to **just under 231 TB of data and 9.7 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In December, **each request to cdnjs used only 23.72 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and angular.js.
  - Across the 31.44 billion requests for jquery,
    846.05 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 30.87 billion requests and consumed 1,612.53 TB
    of bandwidth, with angular.js assets in the top 100 getting 12.98 billion requests and
    using 163.79 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.03 billion requests, and
    gsap in 5th with 6.47 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 20.61 billion requests and using 557.63 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    7.03 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 4.75 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 3,016,999,808 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 301,699,980,800 requests in December.

**Nearly 302 billion requests or roughly 9.7 billion requests each day of December**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 71,569.29 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 7,156,929.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 7.16 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 7.16 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
