Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (July 2025), cdnjs served **nearly 289 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 7.03 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **nearly 227 TB of data and 9.3 billion requests each day** (averaged). 🤯
- In July, **each request to cdnjs used only 24.34 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 29.54 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,441.06 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 28.79 billion requests and consumed 802.13 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 7.88 billion requests and
    using 48.78 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - slick-carousel came in 4th this month with 6.30 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 5.78 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 18.22 billion requests and using 510.81 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    7.88 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.18 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,886,711,822 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 288,671,182,200 requests in July.

**Nearly 289 billion requests or around 9.3 billion requests every single day of July**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 70,267.69 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 7,026,769.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 7.03 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 7.03 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
