## Editor's note

ðŸ‘‹ This month we observed a substantial drop in the total requests to cdnjs. This appears to mainly be a reduction in requests specifically to the `/cdn-cgi/trace` endpoint as well as to jQuery 3.3.1 (`jquery/3.3.1/jquery.min.js`), which has dropped from being the top resource on cdnjs to 31st! We've confirmed with Cloudflare that this data is accurate and this appears to be a legitimate reduction in requests, likely due to a single large website/service no longer using these two resources from the CDN.

## Key highlights

- This month (April 2025), cdnjs served **over 274 billion requests**. ðŸ–¥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 6.09 PB of data** to serve these requests. ðŸ“¤
- That works out to **just under 203 TB of data and 9.1 billion requests each day** (averaged). ðŸ¤¯
- In April, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 22.19 KB of data**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 26.46 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,165.17 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 20.97 billion requests and consumed 562.41 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 8.79 billion requests and
    using 46.60 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 7.12 billion requests, and
    slick-carousel in 5th with 5.48 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 10.70 billion requests and using 288.84 TB

  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    8.79 billion requests, followed by font-awesome

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,742,961,687 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 274,296,168,700 requests in April.

**Over 274 billion requests or approximately 9.1 billion requests every day of April**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 60,878.33 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,087,833.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.09 PB.
