## Editor's note

👋 Before diving into the data for this month, I just wanted to note that the top resource on cdnjs this month (and last month) was in fact `/cdn-cgi/trace`, a route provided by the Cloudflare proxy layer that returns basic information about the request include IP address. I'm not sure why this is the top, but it is. In the top 100 resources that Cloudflare provides me with, the Polyfill.io replacement service that cdnjs operates also features, so the list towards the end of this document doesn't quite include the full 100 resources it proclaims (as it only includes actual `/ajax/libs` CDN resources).

## Key highlights

- This month (March 2025), cdnjs served **over 318 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 7.30 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That's **over 235 terabytes of data and 10.3 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In March, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 22.93 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 34.85 billion requests for jquery,
    1,703.25 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 26.28 billion requests and consumed 1,114.62 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 9.08 billion requests and
    using 46.88 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 7.26 billion requests, and
    angular.js in 5th with 6.09 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 15.18 billion requests and using 1,175.38 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    10.24 billion requests, followed by webfont
    (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) with 9.08 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 3,182,965,067 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 318,296,506,700 requests in March.

**Over 318 billion requests or around 10.3 billion requests each day of March**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 72,996.21 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 7,299,621.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 7.30 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 7.30 petabytes of data for requests in March**. 🤯
