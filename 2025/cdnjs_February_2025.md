## Key highlights

- This month (February 2025), cdnjs served **over 298 billion requests**. ðŸ–¥
- This month, cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 6.73 PB of data** to serve these requests. ðŸ“¤
- That's **over 240 terabytes of data and 10.6 billion requests each day** (averaged). ðŸ¤¯
- In February, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 22.57 KB of data**. ðŸ”
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome,
  and webfont.
  - Across the 35.07 billion requests for jquery,
    1,689.69 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 24.21 billion requests and consumed 1,022.27 TB
    of bandwidth, with webfont assets in the top 100 getting 8.66 billion requests and
    using 44.19 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 6.90 billion requests, and
    slick-carousel in 5th with 5.96 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.3.1 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 14.97 billion requests and using 1,155.97 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.6.0/jquery.min.js) came in second with
    11.06 billion requests, followed by webfont
    (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) with 8.66 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,980,544,081 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 298,054,408,100 requests in February.

**Over 298 billion requests or approximately 10.6 billion requests every day of February**. ðŸ“ˆ

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 67,270.14 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,727,014.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.73 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 6.73 petabytes of data for requests in February**. ðŸ¤¯
