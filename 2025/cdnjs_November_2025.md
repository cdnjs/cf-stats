Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (November 2025), cdnjs served **nearly 297 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 6.94 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **over 231 TB of data and 9.9 billion requests each day** (averaged). 🤯
- In November, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 23.41 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and angular.js.
  - Across the 30.73 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,577.13 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 24.95 billion requests and consumed 669.32 TB
    of bandwidth, with angular.js assets in the top 100 getting 12.83 billion requests and
    using 163.10 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.86 billion requests, and
    gsap in 5th with 6.65 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 14.16 billion requests and using 381.22 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    7.86 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 4.86 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,966,180,698 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 296,618,069,800 requests in November.

**Nearly 297 billion requests or around 9.9 billion requests every day of November**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 69,441 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,944,100.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.94 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 6.94 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
