# cdnjs August 2021 Usage Stats

Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (August 2021), cdnjs served **nearly 214 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 3.94 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That's **over 127 terabytes of data and 6.9 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In August, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 18.43 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome, and gsap.
  - Across the 15.30 billion requests for jquery, 610.00 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 14.65 billion requests and consumed 467.65 TB of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100
    getting 9.55 billion requests and using 265.11 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.76 billion requests, and jquery-mousewheel in 5th with 4.28 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of webfont, reaching a total of 7.76
  billion requests and using 42.38 TB of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - font-awesome (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) came in second with 5.71 billion requests, followed by jquery
    (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) with 5.51 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,137,463,298 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 213,746,329,800 requests in August.

**Nearly 214 billion requests or roughly 6.9 billion requests every single day of August**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 39,395.9 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 3,939,590.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 3.94 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 3.94 petabytes of data for requests in August**. 🤯
