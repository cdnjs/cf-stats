Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (September 2021), cdnjs served **nearly 220 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 4.06 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **over 135 TB of data and 7.3 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In September, **each request to cdnjs used only 18.50 KB of data on average**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were jquery, font-awesome, and gsap.
  - Across the 15.87 billion requests for jquery, 654.06 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - font-awesome received 14.62 billion requests and consumed 467.70 TB of bandwidth, with gsap assets in the top 100
    getting 10.38 billion requests and using 286.85 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 7.80 billion requests, and jquery-mousewheel in 5th with 4.12 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was webfontloader.js from version 1.6.28 of webfont, reaching a total of 7.80
  billion requests and using 42.55 TB of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - jquery (3.3.1/jquery.min.js) came in second with 6.18 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 5.67 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,196,189,858 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 219,618,985,800 requests in September.

**Nearly 220 billion requests or roughly 7.3 billion requests every single day of September**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 40,622.88 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 4,062,288.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 4.06 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 4.06 petabytes of data for requests this month**. 🤯
