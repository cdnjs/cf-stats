## Key highlights

- cdnjs served **nearly 266 billion requests** in February 2026. 🖥
- cdnjs used **a massive consumption of 6.32 PB of data** to serve these requests this month. 📤
- That's **nearly 226 terabytes of data and 9.5 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In February, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 23.78 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and angular.js.
  - Across the 30.10 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,573.53 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 24.80 billion requests and consumed 665.53 TB
    of bandwidth, with angular.js assets in the top 100 getting 11.41 billion requests and
    using 147.23 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - webfont came in 4th this month with 6.78 billion requests, and
    gsap in 5th with 6.21 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 15.71 billion requests and using 424.56 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    6.78 billion requests, followed by font-awesome
    (4.7.0/css/font-awesome.min.css) with 4.25 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,656,228,283 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 265,622,828,300 requests in February.

**Nearly 266 billion requests or roughly 9.5 billion requests every day of February**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 63,174.26 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,317,426.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.32 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a massive bandwidth consumption of 6.32 petabytes of data for requests in February**. 🤯
