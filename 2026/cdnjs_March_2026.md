Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key highlights

- This month (March 2026), cdnjs served **over 291 billion requests**. 🖥
- This month, cdnjs used **a huge consumption of 6.97 PB of data** to serve these requests. 📤
- That works out to **nearly 225 TB of data and 9.4 billion requests every day** (averaged). 🤯
- In March, **each request to cdnjs (on average) used only 23.89 KB of data**. 🔍
 
### Library highlights

- The top libraries overall (in the top 100 assets) this month were font-awesome, jquery,
  and angular.js.
  - Across the 33.27 billion requests for font-awesome,
    1,761.10 TB of bandwidth was consumed.
  - jquery received 26.89 billion requests and consumed 722.20 TB
    of bandwidth, with angular.js assets in the top 100 getting 14.62 billion requests and
    using 186.31 TB of bandwidth to be served.
  - gsap came in 4th this month with 7.42 billion requests, and
    webfont in 5th with 6.66 billion.
- The top asset on the CDN this month was jquery.min.js from version 3.6.0 of
  jquery, reaching a total of 17.17 billion requests and using 463.79 TB
  of bandwidth to serve the file.
  - webfont (1.6.28/webfontloader.js) came in second with
    6.66 billion requests, followed by angular.js
    (1.6.4/angular.min.js) with 4.70 billion requests.

## Total number of requests

> The first important stat that we are given is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.
> 
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 2,914,969,756 at 1%.

When multiplied up to 100%, this results in cdnjs serving approximately 291,496,975,600 requests in March.

**Over 291 billion requests or roughly 9.4 billion requests each day of March**. 📈

## Total bandwidth usage

> Another great stat that Cloudflare has given us again is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.
> 
> This number, like total requests, is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes: 69,651.12 GB.

When multiplied up to be 100%, this produces the estimate of 6,965,112.0 GB of bandwidth used for this month by
 cdnjs, or 6.97 PB.

**This gives cdnjs a huge bandwidth consumption of 6.97 petabytes of data for requests in March**. 🤯
